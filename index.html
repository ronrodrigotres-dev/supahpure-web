THE TREASURES OF MARTA

a 2D animated series

Key Art

Three misfit scouts, a very passionate guide and an old logbook. What could go wrong on this treasure hunt adventure?
The Treasures of Marta follows three young scouts who, guided by the legendary Marta’s journal, embark on thrilling adventures to uncover hidden treasures beneath the modern city. Along the way, they discover secrets from the past, face unexpected challenges, and learn the importance of friendship and teamwork.

Director: Antonia Venegas Gonzalez & Marin Sanchez Olguin

Writer: Lisette Kamann Beuchat

Producers: Rodrigo Venegas Vargas

Episodes: 12 x 11

Target: 8 to 10
